### define saviour
`[seyv-yer]`  

Word Origin  
noun  

1. a person who saves, rescues, or delivers: 'the savior of the country.'
 
2. (initial capital letter) a title of God, especially of Christ.  

3. (initial capital letter) Classical Mythology. an epithet of Artemis.
